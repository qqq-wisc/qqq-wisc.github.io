The Qool Quantum Compiler Project

Welcome to the Qool Quantum Qompiler project (QQQ) from the University of Wisconsin–Madison. We’re working on a robust end-to-end compiler for quantum programs to keep up with a rapidly evolving hardware landscape. You can find our software at our Github organization.

We’re giving a tutorial at ASPLOS 2025 on our work! Join us!
